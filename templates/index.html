About

We have created a program that automatically creates a customized and personalized timetable with certain inputs from users. Timetables prevent waste of time and energy: The timetable shows exactly what is to be done at a particular time. It, thus, directs the attention of the teacher to one thing at a time. Thus, one's energy is automatically directed in a particular direction which prevents waste of time and energy.
